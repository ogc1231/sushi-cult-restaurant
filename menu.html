- Our Food -

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest fish caught sustainably in Irish waters and we source only the best seasonal ingredients.

Sashimi

Raw fish, served in long rectangular slices known as hira-zukuri.

Nigiri

Molded structures of vinegared rice topped with slices of raw fish.

Chirashi

Bed of sushi rice with fish and vegetables scattered over the top

Maki

Bite-size pieces wrapped in a thin layer of nori (seaweed).

Uramaki

Similar to Maki, with the rice on the outside of the nori rather than the inside.

Temaki

Cone of Nori filled with rice, vegetables, and fish. Also known as hand rolls.

All our dishs are freshly made to order to ensure only the highest standards freshness and taste.
