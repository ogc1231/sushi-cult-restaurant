- About -

Sushi Cult is the fusion of the highest quality Irish ingredient and acient Japenese technique. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest fish caught sustainably in Irish waters and we source only the best seasonal ingredients

- Story -

"Growing up in Howth with chef as a mother I quickly learnt about the quality of Irish produce, which somehow felt under appreciated. After many trips to Japan to visit my father's family, I began to learn about the culture of becoming a master in one discipline.

So when I turned 18 I moved to Japan to study to become a sushi Itamae (chef). After 10 years of training and working in some of the best sushi resturants across Japan, I returned home to put my spin on the iconic Japanese cusine. I hope you enjoy."
